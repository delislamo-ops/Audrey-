Garlic Tomato Spaghetti

Fresh tomato-garlic sauce with silky texture.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Ginger

Instructions

Boil Cook tomatoes and garlic until soft.
Blend Blend with cooking water into smooth sauce.
Cook Pasta Boil spaghetti and save pasta water.
Combine Simmer sauce, add pasta water, mix with spaghetti.
