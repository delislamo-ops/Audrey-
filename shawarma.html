Oven Chicken Shawarma

Juicy marinated chicken with creamy garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Olive oil
  • Hot pepper paste
  • Greek yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Mustard
  • Salt, pepper
  • Paprika, cumin, garlic powder, ginger

Instructions

Marinate Mix all marinade ingredients and coat chicken. Refrigerate overnight.
Bake Cover with foil and bake at 200°C until tender, then uncover to brown.
Garlic Sauce Blend garlic, yogurt, lemon, mustard, and oil until smooth.
Finish Brush chicken with juices and toast in wrap for extra flavor.
