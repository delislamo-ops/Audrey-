Pan Seared Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (or breast)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken stock (optional)

Steps

Pat chicken dry completely for a better sear. Season with salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Heat a pan with a mix of olive oil and a bit of butter. Place chicken skin-side down and don’t move it until golden and crispy. Flip and cook the other side until browned. Add a splash of chicken stock and cover to finish cooking through. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
