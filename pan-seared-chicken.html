Crispy Seared Chicken

Juicy inside with crispy, flavorful skin.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp Butter
  • Optional: Chili flakes

The Instructions

Step 1: Season Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, and optional chili flakes. Coat evenly.
Step 2: heat the pan Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat until hot.
Step 3: sear Place chicken in the pan and cook 5–6 minutes per side without moving it too much.
Step 4: Add Flavor Add garlic and butter in the last 2 minutes. Spoon melted butter over chicken.
Step 5: Rest Let chicken rest 5 minutes before slicing to keep it juicy.
