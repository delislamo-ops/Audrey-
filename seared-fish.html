Seared Fish Fillet

Crispy outside, tender and juicy inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 Fish Fillets (white fish)
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Lemon
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Fresh Parsley

The Instructions

Step 1: Prep Pat the fish completely dry using paper towels. Season generously with salt, pepper, and paprika.
Step 2: Sear Heat olive oil in a pan until shimmering. Place fish presentation-side down and DO NOT TOUCH for 3–4 minutes to get a golden crust.
Step 3: Butter Baste Flip the fish. Add butter, garlic, and spoon the melted butter over the fish continuously.
Step 4: Finish Squeeze fresh lemon juice on top and sprinkle parsley.
