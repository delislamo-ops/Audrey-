Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. In a pot, melt butter, add flour and cook until paste forms. Slowly add milk while stirring until smooth. Add cheese and melt until creamy. Mix with pasta and bake for extra texture.
