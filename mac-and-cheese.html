Classic Mac & Cheese

Creamy, dreamy, and baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • 16oz Elbow Macaroni
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup Flour
  • 3 cups Whole Milk
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 4 cups Sharp Cheddar
  • 2 cups Gruyere
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • Salt & Black Pepper

The Instructions

Step 1: The Pasta Boil your pasta in heavily salted water. Cook it for 2 minutes less than the box says (Al Dente). It needs to be firm because it will soak up sauce in the oven later.
Step 2: The Roux In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for about 2 minutes. This creates a "roux" which thickens the sauce and makes it professional-grade.
Step 3: The Cream Slowly pour in the milk and heavy cream while whisking. Keep stirring until it thickens up enough to coat the back of a spoon. Turn the heat to low.
Step 4: The Cheese Add 5 cups of the shredded cheese and all your spices. Stir until it's a smooth, cheesy liquid. Fold in your cooked pasta until every noodle is covered.
Step 5: The Bake Pour everything into a baking dish. Sprinkle the last cup of cheese on top. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes until the top is bubbling and slightly crispy.
