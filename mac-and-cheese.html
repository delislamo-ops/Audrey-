Mac & Cheese

PREP: 15 MINS | COOK: 30 MINS | SERVES: 4-6

What You'll Need

  • 1lb Cavatappi or Elbow Pasta
  • 1/2 cup Salted Butter
  • 1/2 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 cups Whole Milk
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 4 cups Sharp White Cheddar
  • 2 cups Smoked Gouda
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

The Process

Step 01 The Al Dente Foundation: Bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a rolling boil. Cook your pasta for exactly 2 minutes less than the package instructions. It should be slightly firm to the bite; it will soften perfectly later in the oven. Drain and set aside.
