Classic Baked Mac & Cheese

The ultimate comfort food with a golden, bubbly crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Elbow Macaroni
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 cup All-purpose Flour
  • 3 cups Whole Milk (warm)
  • 4 cups Sharp Cheddar (grated)
  • 1 cup Gruyère or Jack Cheese
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder

Instructions

  1. Boil: Cook pasta in heavily salted water for 2 minutes less than the package directions. Drain and toss with a splash of oil to prevent sticking.
  2. The Roux: In a large heavy saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until it smells slightly nutty but hasn't browned.
  3. The Sauce: Slowly stream in milk while whisking constantly. Simmer until the sauce thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon.
  4. The Cheese: Remove from heat. Fold in 3 cups of cheese and spices. Stir until completely smooth and velvety.
  5. Bake: Fold the pasta into the sauce. Pour into a greased baking dish and top with the remaining cheese. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes until the top is golden and bubbling.
