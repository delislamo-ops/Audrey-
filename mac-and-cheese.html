The Ultimate Mac & Cheese

Extra creamy, triple-cheese, and baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Elbow Macaroni
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 cup All-purpose Flour
  • 3 cups Whole Milk
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 4 cups Sharp Cheddar (grated)
  • 2 cups Gruyère Cheese
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • Panko Breadcrumbs (for topping)

The Process

01. Prep the Pasta: Boil the macaroni in heavily salted water. Aim for "slightly underdone" (about 2 minutes less than the box says). They will finish cooking in the oven.

02. The Golden Roux: Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for 2 minutes until it smells like toasted cookies. This prevents a "floury" taste.

03. Build the Sauce: Slowly pour in the milk and heavy cream while whisking constantly. Simmer until the sauce is thick enough to coat the back of a spoon.

04. The Melt: Turn off the heat. Stir in 5 cups of the cheese (save 1 cup for the top) until the sauce is a smooth, velvet river of gold.

05. The Bake: Fold the pasta into the sauce. Pour into a baking dish, top with remaining cheese and panko. Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes until the top is bubbling and crispy.
