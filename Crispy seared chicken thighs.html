Crispy Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
  • Salt (for dry brine)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp ginger
  • Black pepper
  • ½ cup chicken stock

Instructions

  • Season chicken generously with salt and let it rest (dry brine) for at least 30 minutes.
  • Place chicken skin-side down in a cold pan with oil, then turn on medium heat.
  • Allow fat to render slowly. Do not move the chicken until it releases naturally and becomes golden and crispy.
  • In a small bowl, mix butter, paprika, garlic powder, ginger, and black pepper.
  • Flip the chicken and coat with the butter mixture.
  • Add chicken stock to the pan.
  • Cover with foil and transfer to oven at 180°C.
  • Cook until fully done and juicy inside.
