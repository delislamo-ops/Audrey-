Chocolate Cake

Rich, moist, and deeply chocolatey.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 ¾ cups Sugar
  • ¾ cup Cocoa Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Milk
  • ½ cup Oil
  • 1 cup Hot Coffee
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • Vanilla Extract

The Instructions

Step 1: Mix Dry Combine flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
Step 2: Add Wet Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
Step 3: Secret Step Slowly pour in hot coffee — this deepens the chocolate flavor.
Step 4: Bake Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–35 minutes.
