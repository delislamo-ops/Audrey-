Creamy Mashed Potatoes

Ultra smooth buttery mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 250g butter
  • 250ml milk
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

Boil Cook potatoes in salted water until soft.
Dry Let steam fully escape before mashing.
Mash Add butter first, then milk gradually.
Finish Pass through a sieve for ultra-smooth texture.
