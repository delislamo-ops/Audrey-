Smash Burger

Crispy edges, juicy center, restaurant-style burger.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt, pepper, ginger
  • Onions
  • Cheese slices
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Sauce

Instructions

Prepare Season beef lightly and form balls.
Smash Press onto hot pan until thin and crispy.
Cook Add onions, flip, and melt cheese.
Assemble Toast buns in beef fat and build burger.
