Mac & Cheese

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (elbow or similar)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk (warm)
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar preferred)
  • Salt & pepper
  • Breadcrumbs (optional)

Instructions

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta until al dente, then drain.
  • In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add flour and whisk continuously for 1–2 minutes to form a roux (smooth paste).
  • Slowly pour in warm milk while whisking to prevent lumps. Continue until smooth and thickened.
  • Add cheese gradually, stirring until fully melted and creamy.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Combine pasta with the sauce and mix thoroughly.
  • Transfer to a baking dish, top with extra cheese and breadcrumbs.
  • Bake at 180°C until golden and slightly crispy on top.
