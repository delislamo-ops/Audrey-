🍗 Crispy Seared Chicken

Golden outside, juicy inside

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp butter

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan on medium-high heat.
  3. Place chicken and sear for 5–6 minutes per side.
  4. Add garlic and butter in the last 2 minutes.
  5. Spoon melted butter over chicken while cooking.
  6. Cook until golden brown and fully done inside.
  7. Rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Tips

Don’t flip the chicken too early — let it form a crust for best crispiness.

Roasted Chicken

Juicy inside with crispy, flavorful skin.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Lemon
  • Rosemary / Thyme
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Paprika

The Instructions

Step 1: Season Rub chicken with oil, salt, pepper, paprika, and herbs. Get under the skin for max flavor.
Step 2: Stuff Stuff cavity with garlic, onion, and lemon halves.
Step 3: Roast Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 1 hour 15 min. Baste halfway through.
Step 4: Rest Let it rest 10 minutes before cutting — keeps it juicy.

Crispy Seared Chicken

Golden crust outside, juicy and tender inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp Butter
  • Optional: Chili flakes

The Instructions

Step 1: Season Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, and optional chili flakes. Coat evenly.
Step 2: Heat Pan Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat until hot.
Step 3: Sear Place chicken in the pan and cook 5–6 minutes per side without moving it too much.
Step 4: Add Flavor Add garlic and butter in the last 2 minutes. Spoon melted butter over chicken.
Step 5: Rest Let chicken rest 5 minutes before slicing to keep it juicy.
