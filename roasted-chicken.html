Roasted Chicken

Juicy brined chicken with spiced butter crust.

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Salt brine (10%)
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Garlic powder
  • Ginger
  • Pepper

Instructions

Brine Soak chicken overnight in salted water with aromatics.
Butter Rub Mix spices with butter and coat chicken.
Bake Cover with foil and roast until tender.
Rest Let it sit before cutting for juiciness.
