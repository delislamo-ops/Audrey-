Seared Fish Fillet

Ingredients

  • 2 fish fillets (white fish like tilapia or cod)
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions

  • Pat the fish completely dry with paper towels (this is key for a good sear).
  • Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  • Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add oil.
  • Place the fish presentation-side down and press lightly for even contact.
  • Do not move it — let it cook until a golden crust forms and it releases naturally.
  • Flip gently, then add butter and garlic to the pan.
  • Spoon the melted butter over the fish continuously (basting).
  • Cook until the fish is opaque and flakes easily.
  • Finish with a squeeze of lemon juice and optional herbs.
